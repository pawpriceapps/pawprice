🐾 PawPrice Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 7, 2026

PawPrice ("we", "us", or "our") operates the PawPrice mobile application and website at pawprice.app.

Information We Collect

PawPrice stores your pet profiles locally on your device using browser storage. We do not collect or transmit personal information to our servers.

How We Use Information

Search queries are processed via the Anthropic API to generate pet food price comparisons. We do not store your search history.

Affiliate Links

PawPrice earns a commission when you purchase through our affiliate links at no extra cost to you. We participate in affiliate programs with Amazon, Chewy, PetSmart, and Petco.

Analytics

We use Vercel Analytics to collect anonymous usage data to improve our service. No personally identifiable information is collected.
